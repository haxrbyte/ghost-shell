01
Don't Panic-Pivot
Networking, Linux, scripting, understanding how systems work — the fundamentals haven't changed. Every tool and every AI sits on top of that knowledge.
02
Work With AI
Use it to speed up recon, automate repetitive analysis, understand unfamiliar code. Fight alongside the machine, not against it.
03
Specialize Where AI Struggles
Organizational politics. Legacy system constraints. Physical engagements. Talking to a non-technical exec about risk.

Human presence, creativity, and judgment — that's where you want to be.
